Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Sash Ventures LLC, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. This policy outlines our commitment to data integrity across all our digital properties.

1. Data Collection Philosophy

We do not collect personal identifiers. Our applications are designed to process data locally on the user's device, ensuring that sensitive information never leaves your personal ecosystem.

2. Corporate Security Standards

All ventures under the Sash Ventures umbrella must adhere to strict end-to-end encryption protocols and Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) requirements.

3. Transparency

Should we introduce services that require account creation, users will be notified via this domain with a full breakdown of any data interaction.
